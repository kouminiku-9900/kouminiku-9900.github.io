Private Vault and Data Disclaimer

Importing a video into Private Vault does not delete the original file. It creates an encrypted copy in the App's data area, and decryption requires a locally generated vault key.

Private Vault reduces risk but cannot eliminate every possibility. Do not use it as the only storage location for important videos or material that requires a high level of confidentiality.

For example, problems affecting macOS or your user account, access to an unlocked device, screen recording, Keychain access, malware, or an unexpected defect may allow another person to discover the Vault or access its contents.

Vault data may be lost, corrupted, permanently unrecoverable, or impossible to decrypt in situations including:

  • loss or reset of the macOS Keychain item or key material
  • deletion of the App's data area (app container)
  • reinstalling macOS, migrating to another Mac, or changing user accounts
  • storage failure, power loss, or forced termination interrupting a write

The Private Vault is not a backup feature. Keep the original file and maintain a separate, tested backup independent of Private Vault. Deleting Private Vault or an individual vault item is irreversible.
